[h1]ME(b) - Asari[/h1]

[i]"Embrace eternity!"[/i]

Adds the [b]asari[/b] from BioWare's Mass Effect series to RimWorld as a [b]vanilla-oriented Biotech xenotype[/b] — no custom race framework, just genes.

[h2]The asari[/h2]

A mono-gendered xenotype, distinctly feminine in appearance, renowned for elegance, psychic prowess and a lifespan measured in centuries.

[list]
[*][b]Female-only[/b] — every asari is born female (via Gender Genes).
[*][b]Extreme longevity[/b] — asari live for close to a millennium (via Lifespan Expectancy Genes): they age slowly, stay fertile far longer, and their life stages stretch to match. Newly generated asari can be anywhere from 18 to a [i]thousand[/i] years old.
[*][b]Matriarchal genome[/b] — children carried by an asari inherit an exact copy of their mother's genes and xenotype. The asari line always breeds true.
[*][b]Enhanced psychic ability[/b] — a natural affinity for the psychic (and, with the ME(b) - Biotics addon, full biotic powers).
[*][b]Asari looks[/b] — blue, purple or green skin, semi-flexible cartilage scalp crests (skin-colored, hidden under helmets thanks to ME(b) - Utilities), and [b]16 asari facial-marking tattoos[/b].
[*][b]Lore-friendly names[/b] — a custom asari name generator (Liara, Benezia, Aria... and many more).
[*][b]Bisexual by nature[/b] — as the lore demands.
[/list]

The xenotype is inheritable and fully playable — start with asari colonists, recruit them, or gene-edit your own.
