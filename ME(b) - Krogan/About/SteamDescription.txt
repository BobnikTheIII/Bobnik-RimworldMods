[h1]ME(b) - Krogan[/h1]

[i]"I am Urdnot Wrex, and this is my planet!"[/i]

Adds the [b]krogan[/b] from BioWare's Mass Effect series to RimWorld as a [b]vanilla-oriented Biotech xenotype[/b] — no custom race framework, just genes.

[h2]The krogan[/h2]

Hulking, hyper-aggressive warriors designed for battle. They thrive where others die — and they never, ever stop coming.

[list]
[*][b]Built for war[/b] — hulk bodies, strong melee damage, robust constitution, reduced pain, unstoppable movement, and strong aptitude for both melee and shooting.
[*][b]Survivors of Tuchanka[/b] — resistant to extreme heat and cold and completely immune to environmental toxins. A krogan walks through a toxic fallout like it's a light drizzle.
[*][b]The genophage[/b] — every krogan carries the [i]genophaged[/i] gene, an engineered affliction that cuts fertility to a tenth. Krogan children are rare and precious, just as the lore demands.
[*][b]A price for power[/b] — hyper-aggressive, psychically deaf, slow learners, mildly unstable, poor at social niceties and hopeless at art and research. Krogan solve problems with a headbutt.
[*][b]Krogan looks[/b] — custom male and female krogan heads, [b]six colors of hard scaled head crests[/b] (white, red, blue, yellow, grey, dark), reptilian skin in five shades, and low guttural roaring voices.
[*][b]Lore-friendly names[/b] — custom male and female krogan name generators (Urdnot, Wrex, Grunt... and many more).
[/list]

The xenotype is inheritable and fully playable — start as a krogan clan, recruit them, or gene-edit your own.
