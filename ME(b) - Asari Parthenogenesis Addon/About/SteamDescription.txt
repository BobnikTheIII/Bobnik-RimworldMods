[h1]ME(b) - Asari Parthenogenesis Addon[/h1]

In the lore of BioWare's [b]Mass Effect[/b] games, the asari reproduce through a form of parthenogenesis — an asari can conceive a daughter with a partner of any species and any gender. This addon brings that missing piece to the [b]ME(b) - Asari[/b] mod: [b]asari can now have children with female partners[/b].

[h2]How it works[/h2]

[list]
[*]Adds the [b]Asari Parthenogenesis[/b] gene, automatically part of the asari xenotype. Any other pawn can also acquire the gene through gene editing.
[*]A gene carrier in a relationship with another woman can [b]get pregnant from lovin'[/b], just like a vanilla opposite-sex couple.
[*]All the vanilla rules still apply — fertility, age, sterility and pregnancy approach are checked exactly like in an ordinary couple, and the same UI messages are used. This is not a "free babies" button.
[*]Combined with the asari [b]Matriarchal Genome[/b], the child is a true asari, inheriting her mother's genes and xenotype.
[/list]

[list]
