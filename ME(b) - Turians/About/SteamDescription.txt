[h1]ME(b) - Turians[/h1]

[i]"I'm Garrus Vakarian, and this is now my favorite spot on the Rim."[/i]

Adds the [b]turians[/b] from BioWare's Mass Effect series to RimWorld as a [b]vanilla-oriented Biotech xenotype[/b] — no custom race framework, just genes.

[h2]The turians[/h2]

The galaxy's most disciplined soldiers: born marksmen with plated hides and a soldier's tolerance for pain.

[list]
[*][b]Best snipers on the Rim[/b] — a remarkable natural aptitude for shooting. Hand a turian a rifle and watch them do calibrations in real time.
[*][b]Plated and hardened[/b] — reduced pain sensitivity; their metallic carapace shrugs off what would drop a baseliner.
[*][b]Soldier's temperament[/b] — natural optimists who keep morale up through the worst tours of duty... but ask them to make art and you'll get a disciplinary report instead.
[*][b]Children of Palaven[/b] — a hot homeworld left them slightly vulnerable to cold. Pack warm clothes for winter deployments.
[*][b]Turian looks[/b] — a custom avian head with mandibles and prominent facial plating (male and female variants), and six skin tones from slate gray and sheer white to blue, brown and green.
[*][b]Lore-friendly names[/b] — custom male and female turian name generators (Garrus, Saren, Victus...).
[/list]

The xenotype is inheritable and fully playable — start a turian frontier outpost, recruit them, or gene-edit your own.
