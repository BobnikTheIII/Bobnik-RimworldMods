[h1]ME(b) - Quarians[/h1]

[i]"Keelah se'lai."[/i]

Adds the [b]quarians[/b] from BioWare's Mass Effect series to RimWorld as a [b]vanilla-oriented Biotech xenotype[/b] — no custom race framework, just genes.

[h2]The quarians[/h2]

Slight of build, quick on their feet, and cursed with an immune system that never was their strong suit.

[list]
[*][b]Weak immune system[/b] — the quarian signature. Disease is dangerous for them; keep a good doctor (and a clean hospital) around. Suit up before wandering the wilds.
[*][b]Surprisingly robust physiology[/b] — despite their frailty to infection, quarian bodies sustain more physical damage than an average baseliner.
[*][b]Quick and light[/b] — faster movement in a slighter, thinner frame, at the cost of weaker melee strikes.
[*][b]Three-fingered hands[/b] — quarian hands have three fingers, slightly hampering complex manipulation.
[*][b]Quarian looks[/b] — bright white glowing eyes, characteristic head lines, and pale, pink or purple skin tones.
[*][b]Lore-friendly names[/b] — custom male and female quarian name generators, with proper clan-style surnames (vas Normandy would be proud).
[/list]

The xenotype is inheritable and fully playable — start a quarian flotilla-in-exile, recruit them, or gene-edit your own.
