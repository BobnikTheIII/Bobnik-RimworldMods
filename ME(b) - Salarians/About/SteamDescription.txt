[h1]ME(b) - Salarians[/h1]

[i]"Had to be me. Someone else might have gotten it wrong."[/i]

Adds the [b]salarians[/b] from BioWare's Mass Effect series to RimWorld as a [b]vanilla-oriented Biotech xenotype[/b] — no custom race framework, just genes.

[h2]The salarians[/h2]

The galaxy's fast-talking scientists: a hyperfast metabolism that keeps them awake, energized and working — and burns their candle at both ends.

[list]
[*][b]Hyperfast metabolism[/b] — salarians barely need sleep and are naturally industrious workers. More hours awake, more hours in the lab.
[*][b]A short, bright life[/b] — the price of that metabolism. Via Lifespan Expectancy Genes, salarians live drastically shorter lives than baseliners: they age fast, and their whole life curve (fertility, life stages, mortality) is compressed to match. Plan your colony around it.
[*][b]Cold-blooded pragmatists[/b] — psychically dull and famously low-libido (salarians consider mating a practical matter of contracts).
[*][b]Salarian looks[/b] — a custom salarian head, tall backward-swept horn crests, thin builds, and a wide palette of nine skin tones from ink black through blue to deep red and orange.
[*][b]Lore-friendly names[/b] — custom male and female salarian name generators (Mordin, Kirrahe, and colleagues).
[/list]

The xenotype is inheritable and fully playable — start a salarian task force, recruit them, or gene-edit your own.
