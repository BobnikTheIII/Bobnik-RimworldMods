[h1]ME(b) - Biotics[/h1]

[i]"I'm a biotic god!"[/i]

Brings [b]Mass Effect biotic powers[/b] to RimWorld as three full [b]Vanilla Psycasts Expanded[/b] paths — [b]30 powers[/b] straight from the trilogy (plus a few Andromeda favorites), from Throw and Barrier all the way to Singularity, Nova and Flare.

Biotics are not just another psycaster reskin: becoming one takes both the right [b]biology[/b] (a gene) and the right [b]hardware[/b] (a brain implant) — just like in Mass Effect.

[h2]Three biotic paths, 10 powers each[/h2]

[b]⚛ Adept — Biotic Control[/b]
Keep enemies helpless. Throw, Pull, Lash, Heavy Throw, Lift, Stasis, Lift Grenade, Biotic Focus, Annihilation Field and the capstone [b]Singularity[/b] — a gravity well that suspends every enemy caught inside it.

[b]⚡ Vanguard — Biotic Assault[/b]
Dive in, hit hard, survive on Barrier. Barrier, Biotic Flux, Biotic Charge, Biotic Leap, Slam, Reinforced Barrier, Shockwave, Heavy Charge, Flare — and [b]Nova[/b]. Charging refreshes your Barrier, so the iconic [b]Charge → Nova[/b] loop works exactly the way you remember it.

[b]🛡 Sentinel — Warp & Wards[/b]
Attrition and protection. Warp, Reave, Lance, Dark Channel, Backlash, Warp Field, Biotic Mending, Biotic Ward, Cluster Ward and Barrier Sphere. Biotic damage-over-time [b]ignores armor[/b] — your anti-mechanoid answer — and [b]Dark Channel jumps to a new victim[/b] when its target dies.

[h2]Becoming a biotic[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Eezo Nodules (gene)[/b] — element zero woven through the nervous system. This is biotic [i]potential[/i]: only carriers can see and unlock the three biotic paths. Acquire it through the gene assembler or extract it from a natural biotic — any pawn can become one.
[*][b]Biotic Amp (implant)[/b] — a craftable Spacer-tech brain implant (research: Biotic Amplification). Installing it grants a psylink and boosts psychic sensitivity, making your powers stronger and last longer.
[*][b]Casting without an amp hurts.[/b] A latent biotic can still force raw mass effect fields through bare nerve tissue — but every cast builds up [b]biotic strain[/b]: pain, then dulled consciousness, fading over a few hours. Install the amp. Trust me.
[/list]

[h2]Balance[/h2]

Every power is a real VPE psycast: costs psyfocus, generates neural heat, and scales with psychic sensitivity. Numbers are calibrated against Vanilla Psycasts Expanded's own abilities — cheap spammable casts at level 1, expensive once-per-fight capstones at level 5, and hard crowd control always costs more than raw damage. Kinetic powers deal blunt damage; warp-type powers tear through armor directly.

[h2]ME(b) series integration[/h2]

Fully standalone — no other ME(b) mod is required. But with [b]ME(b) - Asari[/b] installed, asari are [b]natural biotics[/b]: every asari is born with the Eezo Nodules gene, exactly as the lore demands.

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]

[list]
[*]Should be safe to add to an existing save (new paths appear for gene carriers).
[*]Adds new defs and one small, tightly-scoped Harmony patch — no vanilla or VPE behavior is overwritten, so conflicts are unlikely.
[*]Non-biotic psycasters are completely unaffected: the biotic paths are unavalaible without the gene.
[/list]
