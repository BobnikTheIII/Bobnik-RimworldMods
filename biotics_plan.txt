A Rimworld mod that adds biotic powers from mass effect series (https://masseffect.fandom.com/wiki/Biotics) to rimworld.
Their adaptation in the game should try to imitate the effect to the extent of the game's abilities.
The mod should rely on the Vanilla Psycasts Expanded mod (https://github.com/Vanilla-Expanded/VanillaPsycastsExpanded) integrating itself into the gameplay aspects it offers.
Please keep in mind balancing the game.
